Welcome to the AI Research Lab

The IEEE/ACM AI Workshops and Research Lab brings together students, faculty, and industry professionals to explore the frontiers of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Organized by IEEE/ACM Student Chapter

These workshops are part of the IEEE/ACM chapter's commitment to providing cutting-edge learning opportunities to Monmouth University students.

Research Areas

Machine Learning

Neural networks, deep learning, and advanced ML algorithms

Natural Language Processing

Language models, chatbots, and text analysis

Computer Vision

Image recognition, object detection, and visual AI

Robotics & AI

Autonomous systems and intelligent agents

Active Projects

AI-Powered Student Assistant

Developing an intelligent chatbot to help students with course selection, academic resources, and campus navigation.

Technologies: LLMs, Python, n8n

Computer Vision for Campus Safety

Real-time object detection system for enhancing campus security and monitoring.

Technologies: YOLO, OpenCV, PyTorch

NLP Research Database

Building an advanced search and analysis tool for academic research papers.

Technologies: Transformers, Vector DBs, Python
